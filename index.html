Intelligence

What is the lifespan of the foliage?
Engineered for longevity. Our UV-stabilized polymers are rated for 5-8 years of direct Texas sun exposure with minimal degradation, and 10+ years for shaded or indoor applications.
Is it fire safe?
Absolutely. We offer commercial-grade, fire-retardant foliage that meets NFPA 701 Method 2, essential for hotel lobbies, restaurants, and high-density housing. Always ask for the test.
How is it installed?
We use a proprietary grid system anchored to your existing wall or a custom-fabricated steel frame. The process is clean, rapid, and non-intrusive.
Does it require maintenance?
Zero organic maintenance. No water, no fertilizer, no trimming. A simple rinse with water once or twice a year is all that is needed to keep dust at bay.
Do you install artificial hedges and living walls in Texas?
Yes. We install artificial hedges, faux hedges, and artificial living walls statewide in Texas, including Austin, Dallas, Houston, and San Antonio.
What is the difference between faux living walls and artificial hedges?
Faux living walls are vertical panels with layered foliage for feature walls and interiors. Artificial hedges are dense privacy panels for fences and facades. We fabricate both for your dimensions.
Can you customize frames and panels?
Yes. We build custom frames and panels to fit your wall, fence, or structure. That includes corner returns, logo recesses, and mixed foliage patterns for a tailored look.
How fast is installation?
Most artificial hedge and faux living wall installs are completed in days, not weeks. We pre-fabricate panels offsite and install with minimal disruption.
Are faux living walls HOA friendly?
They are typically HOA friendly because they are non-invasive, do not require irrigation, and keep a consistent appearance. We provide photos and specs to support HOA approvals.
Do you offer acoustic options?
Yes. We can add acoustic backing to artificial hedge and artificial living wall systems to reduce noise for alleys, patios, and equipment screens.
