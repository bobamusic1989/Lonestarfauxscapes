How We Install Artificial Living Walls

Our artificial living wall installation process ensures a seamless result with minimal disruption to your space. From initial consultation to final walkthrough, we handle every detail.

01–3 Days to Install
010+ Year Indoor Lifespan
0$0 Irrigation Cost

Site Consultation & Measurement

We visit your location to assess wall conditions, take precise measurements, and discuss foliage preferences. We photograph the space for design mockups and identify any structural considerations.

Custom Design & Fabrication

Our team creates CAD drawings showing panel layouts and foliage patterns. Once approved, we fabricate powder-coated frames and pre-populate panels with your selected greenery mix in our Texas shop.

Professional Installation

Pre-built panels arrive ready to mount. Our installers secure frames to your wall, connect panels with hidden fasteners, and ensure foliage flows seamlessly across every seam and corner.

Final Walkthrough & Care Guide

We inspect every detail with you, adjust any foliage placement, and provide a simple maintenance guide. Most installations complete in 1-3 days depending on wall size.

Where Artificial Living Walls Work Best

Artificial living walls transform any vertical surface into a maintenance-free green feature. Here are the most popular applications we install across Texas:

Corporate Lobbies & Reception Areas

Create an impressive first impression with a branded living wall behind your reception desk. Logo integration and custom foliage patterns reinforce your company identity while adding biophilic warmth.

Multifamily Amenity Spaces

Apartment and condo amenity decks, gyms, and lounges benefit from artificial living walls. Fire-rated options meet building codes while acoustic backing reduces noise in high-traffic areas.

Restaurants & Hospitality Venues

Enhance dining experiences with lush green backdrops. Our living walls work in kitchens and bars where real plants struggle with grease and low light. LED-ready frames support mood lighting.

Retail Storefronts & Pop-Ups

Attract foot traffic with an eye-catching green wall display. Quick installation means minimal store downtime. Panels can be relocated for seasonal promotions or new store layouts.

Living Wall Foliage Options

We offer a variety of artificial foliage styles to match your design vision. All options are UV-stabilized for outdoor use and available with fire-rated treatments.

Mixed Tropical

Our most popular option combines ferns, philodendrons, and pothos for a lush, layered look. Varying leaf sizes create depth and visual interest suited to modern commercial spaces.

Succulent Mosaic

Geometric patterns using artificial succulents, echeveria, and sedum. Perfect for contemporary designs that want color variation and sculptural texture.

Boxwood & Ivy Blend

A classic combination with dense boxwood base and trailing ivy accents. This timeless style works well in traditional settings and formal entryways.

Custom Botanical Mix

We source specific plants to match your brand colors or existing landscaping. From flowering accents to specific leaf shapes, we build exactly what you envision.
