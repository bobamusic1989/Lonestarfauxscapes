Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with Woodlands covenants?

Yes. We're familiar with The Woodlands Township standards and can provide documentation, renderings, and specifications for covenant approval in all village associations.

Can installations blend with existing trees?

Absolutely. Our custom approach allows us to design around existing trees and landscaping. We select foliage that complements The Woodlands' natural setting.

How do you handle wooded lots?

We specialize in working with The Woodlands' unique tree-covered properties. Our installation crews are experienced with limited-access lots and careful tree protection.

What's the timeline for a project?

After design approval, fabrication takes 1-2 weeks. Most residential installations complete in 1-2 days; commercial projects typically take 2-5 days depending on scope.

Do you offer natural-looking foliage?

Yes. We offer multiple foliage styles designed to look natural and blend with The Woodlands' forest environment—from deep green boxwood to mixed tropical varieties.

How do I get started?

Contact us for a free consultation. We'll visit your Woodlands property, discuss your vision, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote—typically within 48 hours.
