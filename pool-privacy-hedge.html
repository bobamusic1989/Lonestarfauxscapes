Why Artificial Hedges Are Perfect for Pool Areas

Pool environments are tough on plants. Chlorine splash, reflected heat, and constant sun exposure kill natural hedges. Artificial hedges thrive in these conditions.

UV-Stabilized for Texas Sun

Our materials are rated for 5-10 years of direct Texas sun exposure. No fading, no brittleness, no degradation—even in the intense reflected heat around pools.

Chlorine & Chemical Resistant

Splash-out, chemical mist, and backwash won't affect our hedges. The polyethylene material shrugs off chlorine, salt, and pool chemicals without discoloring.

Instant Privacy

No waiting 3-5 years for plants to mature. Your privacy screen is fully effective from the moment we install it. Swim in peace starting day one.

Zero Pool Maintenance Impact

Nothing falls in the water. No leaves, no flowers, no berries, no debris. Your pool stays cleaner and your filtration system works less.

No Pest Attraction

Natural hedges attract bees, wasps, and mosquitoes—the last things you want near your pool. Artificial hedges don't flower, don't hold water, and don't attract pests.

Looks Great Year-Round

No seasonal die-back, no brown patches in August, no bare spots in winter. Your privacy hedge looks lush and green 365 days a year.

Pool Privacy Hedge FAQs

Common questions about artificial privacy hedges for pool areas.

Will artificial hedges hold up near a chlorinated pool?

Yes. Our artificial hedges are made from UV-stabilized polyethylene that resists chlorine, salt, and pool chemicals. The materials won't fade, discolor, or degrade from pool chemical exposure. Many of our installations are directly adjacent to pools and hot tubs with no issues.

How tall can pool privacy hedges be?

We custom-build pool privacy hedges to any height you need. Common heights are 6-8 feet for blocking second-story views, but we can go taller for multi-story neighbors or unique situations. We'll assess your sightlines and recommend the optimal height during your free consultation.

Do artificial hedges drop leaves in the pool?

No. Unlike natural hedges that constantly shed leaves, flowers, and debris into your pool, artificial hedges produce zero debris. No more skimming leaves, no clogged filters, no staining from organic matter. Your pool stays cleaner with significantly less maintenance.

Can pool privacy hedges block views from two-story neighbors?

Yes. We regularly install taller privacy screens (8+ feet) specifically to block elevated sightlines from two-story homes, balconies, and decks. During your consultation, we'll identify all potential sightlines and design a solution that provides complete privacy from every angle.

Will my HOA approve a pool privacy hedge?

Most HOAs approve artificial hedges because they maintain a consistent, well-kept appearance. We provide documentation including product specs, photos of completed installations, and material information to support your HOA submission. Learn more about HOA approval.

How long does installation take?

Most residential pool privacy installations are completed in 1-2 days. We pre-fabricate panels in our shop, so on-site work is primarily mounting and finishing. Your pool area remains accessible throughout the process.
