Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with Frisco HOAs?

Absolutely. We work with HOAs throughout Frisco's master-planned communities and can provide all documentation, renderings, and specifications needed for architectural review approval.

How custom are your installations?

Every project is 100% custom. We measure your exact space, design around your requirements, fabricate custom frames, and select foliage that matches your vision. No two projects are alike.

Can you work around pool equipment?

Yes. We frequently design privacy screens that conceal pool equipment while maintaining access for maintenance. Custom cutouts and removable panels are available.

What's the timeline for a typical project?

After consultation and design approval, fabrication takes 1-2 weeks. Most residential installations complete in 1-2 days; commercial projects typically take 2-5 days.

Do you offer different foliage styles?

Yes. We offer multiple foliage options from traditional boxwood to tropical varieties. During consultation, we'll show samples to find the perfect match for your property's aesthetic.

How do I get started?

Contact us for a free consultation. We'll visit your Frisco property, discuss your vision, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote—typically within 48 hours.
