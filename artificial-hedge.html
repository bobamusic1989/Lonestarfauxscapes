Built to Your Fence, Not From a Box

Getting your artificial hedge right starts with accurate measurements. Here's what we need to provide a precise quote:

Sizing & Measurement Guide
1

Linear Footage

Measure the total length of your fence line, wall, or perimeter. Include corners and returns. We'll calculate panel quantities and fabricate to exact widths.

2

Height Requirements

Measure from ground (or fence top) to your desired privacy height. Consider what you're screening: neighbor windows, equipment, or street views.

3

Mounting Surface

Let us know what we're attaching to: existing fence, block wall, metal posts, or building facade. This determines frame design and fastener type.

4

Site Photos

Photos help us spot challenges: uneven ground, obstacles, access issues. Take wide shots and close-ups of mounting surfaces for the most accurate quote.

Once we have these four details, we size frames, engineer taller runs, and price any fire-rated or acoustic options.

Maintenance & Care

Artificial hedges require minimal maintenance compared to live plants, but some basic care extends their lifespan:

Time Commitment: Just 2-3 cleanings per year compared to weekly maintenance for live hedges.

Cleaning

Rinse with a garden hose 2-3 times per year to remove dust and pollen. In high-traffic or dusty areas near roads, clean quarterly. No chemicals needed.

Debris Removal

Clear leaves, twigs, and debris that collect in the foliage. Use a leaf blower on low setting or remove by hand. Trapped debris can hold moisture and cause staining.

Inspection

Check fasteners and frames annually, especially after major storms. Look for any panels that may have shifted. We offer maintenance visits if needed.

UV Protection

Our foliage is UV-stabilized at the factory. No additional treatments or sprays are needed. Color retention is typically 5-8 years in direct Texas sun.
