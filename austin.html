FAQ — Austin

How fast can you install in Austin?
Most Austin installs are completed in 2–4 days after measurements. Rooftop work is staged to minimize crane or lift time.
Are the hedges fire-rated?
Yes. We offer NFPA 701 Method 2-rated foliage and provide documentation on request for hospitality and multifamily projects.

Both. We coordinate with GCs, designers, or owners to deliver shop drawings, framing plans, and quick installs.
Can you add logos or lighting?
We fabricate frames for backlit logos, signage recesses, and concealed wiring so brand elements sit flush.
