Frequently Asked Questions

How custom are your installations?

Every project is 100% custom. We measure your exact space, design around your specific requirements, fabricate custom frames, and select foliage that matches your vision. No two projects are alike.

Do you work with Plano HOAs?

Yes. We regularly work with HOAs throughout Plano and can provide documentation, renderings, and specifications needed for architectural review approval.

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects in Plano are completed in 1-2 days. Larger commercial installations typically take 2-5 days depending on scope and access requirements.

What's the lead time for custom projects?

After design approval, custom fabrication typically takes 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation based on project complexity.

Can you match specific foliage colors?

Absolutely. We offer multiple foliage styles from bright spring greens to deeper forest tones. During your consultation, we'll show you samples to find the perfect match for your property.

Do you handle permits in Plano?

Most residential hedge installations don't require permits, but we'll advise you during consultation. For commercial projects, we provide all documentation needed for building department approval.
